Data policy

YoungTekkie is designed to work as a static website. Most features run fully in your browser. This page explains what data is stored and where.

What we store

  • Child profiles and progress (e.g., selected track, completed lessons, certificates).
  • Settings such as Kid mode on/off and light UI preferences.

Where it is stored

This site stores information locally on your device using browser storage (often called localStorage). It stays on the device/browser you used and is not automatically shared with us.
